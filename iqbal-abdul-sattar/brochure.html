From a personal passion to the UAE’s luxury fragrance house

The Master Perfumer

With over 45 years in the perfumery industry, Mohamed Iqbal built Khadlaj from the ground up out of a deep, personal passion for authentic, handcrafted fragrance rooted in Middle Eastern traditions. His expertise spans both exquisite natural essences and meticulously crafted synthetic compounds.

The Khadlaj House

Officially founded January 1997 in the UAE as a fully family-owned establishment. Headquartered at Industrial Area 11, Sharjah, with a Dubai retail store in the heart of Souq Murshid — the brand has remained artisanal and family-run for nearly three decades.

Iconic Creations

Hareem Al Sultan — viral global sensation on TikTok & Instagram. Bukhoor Al Bahaar, Oud Pure, Musk Pure — signatures crafted by the master himself.

Family Legacy

Carrying the legacy forward is Mohamed Iqbal’s son Asif Mohamed Iqbal Katchi, current Managing Director / CEO with 18+ years of experience — who led the brand’s expansion to 103+ countries and 20 luxury showrooms across the UAE & Oman.
