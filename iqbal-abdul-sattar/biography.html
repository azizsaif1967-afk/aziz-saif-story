Chapter 01 · The Founder

The visionary Master Perfumer

45 years in fragrance

Mohamed Iqbal Abdul Sattar is the visionary founder and master perfumer of Khadlaj Perfumes, one of the UAE’s most celebrated luxury fragrance houses. With over 45 years of experience in the perfumery industry, he built Khadlaj from the ground up out of a deep, personal passion for authentic, handcrafted fragrance rooted in Middle Eastern traditions.

His expertise spans both exquisite natural essences and meticulously crafted synthetic compounds. He is known for his ardent passion for ingredients such as Musk, Ruh Gulaab, Oud and Vetiver — the building blocks of authentic Arabian perfumery.

Hands-on, from day one

Mohamed Iqbal started hands-on, learning deeply from the rich perfumery traditions of the Gulf and surrounding region, before channelling that knowledge into building a lasting brand.

His commitment to in-house quality control has never wavered — every fragrance is conceived, created, developed and manufactured in the Khadlaj factory, ensuring artisanal integrity at industrial scale.

“Khadlaj started out as a passion and a dream — to create unique and unforgettable fragrances that cater to all preferences and diverse fragrance cultures globally.” — Mohamed Iqbal Abdul Sattar, Founder
Chapter 02 · The Brand

Khadlaj Origin · 1997 to today

Mohamed Iqbal officially founded Khadlaj in January 1997 in the UAE, making it a fully family-owned establishment from day one. He envisioned a brand that could cater to all fragrance preferences and diverse fragrance cultures globally — a blend of Arabic and French perfume artistry under one house.

Headquartered at Industrial Area 11, Sharjah, with a second retail address in Deira, Dubai (Al Khaiyam Hotel Building, Sikkat Al Khail Road), the brand has grown over nearly three decades while keeping its artisanal, family-run identity intact. The Sharjah factory was strategically located in the heart of Dubai’s oldest perfume souq, Souq Murshid, serving both wholesale and retail customers.

Chapter 03 · The Craft

The Master’s Building Blocks

Mohamed Iqbal’s expertise spans both exquisite natural essences and meticulously crafted synthetic compounds. Four ingredients form the heart of his Arabian perfumery practice:

Animalic · Base Note

Musk

The deep, sensual foundation of authentic Arabian perfumery — warmth, longevity, intimacy.

Floral · Heart Note

Ruh Gulaab

Pure rose attar — the signature floral of the Mughal-Arabian tradition. Hand-distilled, intensely concentrated.

Resinous · Crown

Oud

The crown jewel of Arabian fragrance. Mohamed Iqbal’s Oud Pure has become an iconic Khadlaj signature.

Earthy · Base Note

Vetiver

Smoky, grounding, masculine — the green-earth counterpoint that anchors the heritage compositions.

Chapter 05 · The Next Generation

The Family Legacy

Asif Mohamed Iqbal Katchi

Carrying the legacy forward is Asif Mohamed Iqbal Katchi (also known as Asif Iqbal), Mohamed Iqbal’s son and the current Managing Director / CEO of Khadlaj Perfumes. With over 18 years of experience, Asif has led the brand’s global expansion and digital transformation.

Under his leadership, Khadlaj expanded to more than 103 countries, built 20 luxury showrooms across the UAE and Oman, and developed a thriving social media presence with over 230,000 followers across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and Facebook.

A family-owned house

Khadlaj has remained a fully family-owned establishment from day one — an artisanal identity preserved through nearly three decades of growth.

The brand is also a Platinum Sponsor at Beautyworld Middle East and a regular exhibitor at international fragrance trade shows — a testament to the bridge built between father’s craft and son’s reach.

Chapter 06 · Worldwide

Global Reach

Khadlaj Perfumes is present in 103+ countries, with its strongest footprint in the GCC, South Asia, Southeast Asia, Europe and the USA. The brand holds trademarks across most continents and its manufacturing plant in Ras Al Khaimah produces up to 15 million units annually across spray, bakhoor, deodorant, air freshener, lotion and roll-on product lines.
