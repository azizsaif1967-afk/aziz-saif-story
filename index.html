Section IV · Process

A practitioner’s process, not a consultant’s deck.

Every engagement follows the same four moves. Short, specific, and executed alongside your team — so the business owns the result, not the memo.

01 Listen

Diagnose — the real picture

Read the numbers, walk the store or the floor, sit with the team. Map the reality against what the founder believes. The two are almost never the same.

02 Sort

Prioritise — the next 90 days

Three highest-leverage moves, sized for what the team can actually absorb. Everything else goes on a parking list. Focus is the product, not a 40-slide strategy.

03 Build

Execute — alongside the team

Install the systems, run the rollouts, train the teams who’ll own it. Not a deck handed over — a working change installed with the people who have to live with it after.

04 Leave

Hand over — your team owns it

SOPs, dashboards, automation scripts, playbooks — written plain, documented, and transferred. The engagement ends with the business running better without me.
