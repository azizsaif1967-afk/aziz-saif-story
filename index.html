My Story

At 18, I Came to Dubai — And Built a Retail Career Across the Gulf

I arrived in Dubai at the age of 18 and joined Sana Fashions as one of the company's first employees. Over the next 30 years, I grew with the brand from a single store to 35 retail chain locations across 5 GCC countries — rising to Vice President of Operations.

I've built and operated businesses across Retail, Franchise, Trade, Wholesale, Shipping, and Services in the UAE, Oman, KSA, Bahrain, Qatar, and Bangladesh. From negotiating mall leases in Riyadh to onboarding franchise partners in Muscat, every challenge sharpened my instinct for what works.

"Implement in Hours. Not Days. In sha Allah."

Today, I combine 37+ years of real-world business expertise with AI automation to help SMEs get set up, scale smartly, and operate without the bloat.
