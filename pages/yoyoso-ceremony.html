About the Ceremony

One afternoon, one signature, one market.

YOYOSO is a global lifestyle-retail brand from the NEP Group. The Master Franchise Agreement signed in this ceremony opened the Saudi Arabia territory — the anchor market for YOYOSO's GCC expansion.

Aziz led the deal end-to-end: market sizing, investor alignment, contract negotiation, and ground-work for the retail roll-out. The ceremony marked the formal handover — investors, brand principals, partners and the execution team all in one room.
