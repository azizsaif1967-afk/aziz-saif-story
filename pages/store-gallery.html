About Le Pearl

Affordable fashion, every mall.

Le Pearl Fashion is the multi-brand retail group behind a growing network of stores in Oman and the UAE — anchoring kids, men's, and women's collections inside prime mall locations and busy high-street centres.

The product mix brings together European kidswear heritage brands, globally licensed characters (Disney, Marvel, Hello Kitty), and in-house modern-cut labels — all curated for Gulf family shoppers.
