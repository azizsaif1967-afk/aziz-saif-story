The Legacy Journey of Mohamed Iqbal Abdul Sattar — Founder & Master Perfumer of Khadlaj Perfumes, Sharjah, one of the UAE’s most celebrated luxury fragrance houses. Forty-five years in authentic Arabian perfumery — from a personal passion for Musk, Ruh Gulaab, Oud and Vetiver, to a family-owned house present in 103+ countries with iconic creations like Hareem Al Sultan, Bukhoor Al Bahaar & Oud Pure. “A passion and a dream.”

Build Process

From brief to live URL

A legacy biography for a master perfumer demands restraint, dignity & brand alignment — the design must honour the heritage, not overshadow it.

Source the story

Compiled biographical milestones, brand timeline, 100+ public-domain links, marketplaces, social handles & press features into a single resource guide.

Editorial architecture

Mapped the 45-year arc into 8 chapters — Biography, Khadlaj Origin, The Master’s Craft, Iconic Creations, Family Legacy, Global Reach, Press & Connect.

Khadlaj-aligned design system

Midnight onyx + signature gold + brand teal on cream parchment — matching Khadlaj’s own brand colours, but with editorial gravitas.

Hand-coded build

Semantic HTML, mobile-first CSS, 8-tile chapter grid with note-card ingredient blocks — zero frameworks.

Public-domain link map

Aggregated all official websites, social profiles, marketplaces, directory listings, press features & video interviews — the complete digital map in one place.

Deploy & SEO

Schema.org Person markup, sitemap-ready, served at the clean URL azizsaif.com/iqbal-abdul-sattar — shareable anywhere.
