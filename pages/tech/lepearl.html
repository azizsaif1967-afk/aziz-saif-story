Build Process

From catalogue PDF to live URL

The starting point was a printed brand catalogue — we rebuilt it as a responsive web experience without losing any of the editorial feel.

Brand & catalogue audit

Reviewed the 16-page printed catalogue, the Instagram presence & the existing online store to map the full brand universe.

Design tokens & system

Navy & gold palette, Playfair Display headers, Inter body — consistent with the catalogue's print direction.

Catalogue digitisation

Each of the 16 catalogue pages rendered as high-res JPGs with lazy-loading & a lightbox viewer for desktop & mobile.

Social & commerce integration

Instagram feed embed, shop-online CTA to lepearlfashion.com & direct-message links to the Oman store.

Deploy & analytics

Pushed to GitHub Pages at le-pearl.com with SSL, sitemap & GA4 tracking.
