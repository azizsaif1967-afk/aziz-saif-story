Shabbir Merchant

The Legacy Journey of Muhammad Shabbir Merchant — Group Chairman of Champion Group, Dubai (the UAE’s most trusted signage & visual-communication house) and Chairman of the Pakistan Business Council, Dubai. Thirty-seven years of building the UAE’s signage standard — from an 800-sqft Deira workshop in 1989 to a 65,000-sqft campus in Dubai Investments Park 2 serving 1,000+ clients. “One arc of light.”

Build Process

From brief to live URL

A legacy biography for a UAE signage pioneer & community chairman demands accuracy, dignity & brand alignment — not a marketing push.

Source the story

Compiled biographical milestones, Champion Group history & 16 press features directly from Gulf News, Khaleej Times, APP Pakistan & PBC Dubai archives.

Editorial architecture

Mapped the 37-year arc into 8 chapters — Biography, Journey, Champion Group, Affiliations, Press, Video, Instagram & Connect — each a tile on the landing page.

Brand-aligned design system

Navy + red + gold palette matching Champion Group identity — editorial magazine feel, heritage-grade typography.

Hand-coded build

Semantic HTML, mobile-first CSS, video medallion with YouTube embed & structured data — zero frameworks.

Print-ready PDFs

Generated 1-page A4 brochure & 5-page full biography PDF with embedded clickable short link — both downloadable from the site.

Deploy & short link

Hosted on GitHub Pages, with a masked redirect at azizsaif.com/shabbirmerchant — shareable anywhere.
