The official legacy biography of Dr. A. Hadi Shahid — Founder & Managing Partner of Alliott Hadi Shahid Chartered Accountants & Consultancy, Abu Dhabi. Fifty years of service to the profession across the UAE, Pakistan & Afghanistan — audit, commercial law, forensic accounting, arbitration & succession management. “A tribute to five decades of excellence.”

Technology Stack

A dignified static site for a 50-year legacy

HTML5, hand-crafted CSS with a navy & gold palette (Cinzel for titles, Lora & Cormorant Garamond for body) and vanilla JavaScript. Schema.org Person markup, Open Graph & Twitter cards for shareable previews. Hosted on GitHub Pages with the custom domain drhadishahid.com.

Build Process

From brief to live URL

A biography site for a senior accounting professional demands accuracy, restraint & timelessness — not flash.

Interview & source archive

Collected biographical milestones, firm history, press clippings & partner portraits directly from the Alliott Hadi Shahid archive.

Information architecture

Mapped the 50-year arc into chapters — early years, ICAP Fellowship, Alliott Global Alliance, philanthropy & succession.

Heritage design system

Navy & gold palette with classical roman titling — legible on print-style parchment background.

Hand-coded build

Semantic HTML, mobile-first CSS & structured data — tested on real devices with slow networks.

Deploy & SEO

Schema.org Person markup, sitemap, robots.txt & canonical tags. Hosted on GitHub Pages with SSL.
