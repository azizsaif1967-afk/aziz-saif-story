Client Brief

The ask

A luxury accessories entrepreneur had a finished product range and manufacturing partner locked in — but no go-to-market plan. Needed a full launch feasibility across retail, gifting and corporate channels.

Problem

Product-ready, strategy-absent

Samples were beautiful, manufacturing was sorted — but pricing, positioning, channel mix and year-1 revenue expectations were gut-feel.

Opportunity

Gift + corporate is untapped

Boutique retail was obvious. Corporate gifting (hotels, airlines, banks) and duty-free were under-priced opportunities — 60%+ of category value in the GCC.

🔒

Approach

How we built it

A 5-step repeatable process — from brief to investor-ready pack, powered by AI across financial modelling, market sizing & narrative writing.

Category & price mapping

Mapped 14 comparable brands across 4 price tiers. Positioned the brand at the AED 480-720 retail sweet spot.

Channel mix & unit economics

Boutique retail, airline/hotel gifting, corporate bulk, DTC website. Each channel has its own margin, volume, and lead-time.

Launch CapEx & working capital

Opening stock 2,400 units, brand development, trade show attendance, PR retainer, sampling programme, first-year opex.

Year 1 P&L & channel targets

Monthly revenue build with seasonal peaks (Eid, Ramadan, Dec gifting). Year 1 target AED 4.6M split 40/35/25 across channels.

Investor pitch & 3-year brand plan

Brand book, pitch deck, channel playbook, Year 1 marketing calendar, full Excel model. Ready for family-office partner conversations.

What the client walked away with

Brand launched 4 months after the feasibility was delivered. Landed 2 of the top 5 gifting targets (a luxury hotel group and a regional bank) in the first 6 months.
