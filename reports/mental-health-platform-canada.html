06 — Client Onboarding Flow

From Download to
First AI Session in 4 Minutes

Fully automated. No human touchpoint required. The AI handles every step of onboarding, personalisation, and ongoing engagement.

User Onboarding (B2C — 14 Steps)
1
Download · App Store or Google Play
App Discovery & Install
User finds [Mental Health Platform] via App Store search ("Canada mental health app"), Google SEO, or social media (TikTok/Reddit organic). Anonymous mode option shown on first screen — no sign-up required to explore.
2
Step 2 · < 30 seconds
Choose Your Journey
Single screen: "What brings you here today?" — 6 icons (Anxiety, Depression, Stress, Loneliness, Sleep, General Wellness). Selection personalises the entire app experience from this point.
3
Step 3 · Optional
Anonymous or Named Account
Email or Apple/Google sign-in — or continue anonymously. Anonymous users get full AI chat but no social feed or mood history sync. Encourages safety-first signup with zero friction.
4
Step 4 · AI-Powered
PHQ-2 + GAD-2 Micro-Assessment
AI guides user through 4 clinically validated questions in a conversational chat format (not a clinical form). Instantly generates a personalised Wellness Profile. Score stored for progress tracking.
5
Step 5 · Personalisation
AI Builds Your Plan
Based on assessment + topic selection, AI generates: (a) a 4-week program, (b) 3 community groups to join, (c) first 3 coping tools. Shown as an animated "your plan is ready" card.
6
Step 6 · First Value Moment
First AI Chat Session Starts Now
AI Companion "Mira" greets user by name (or "friend" if anonymous), references their chosen topic, and starts the first conversation. Goal: deliver insight or relief within 5 minutes of first launch.
7
Step 7 · Community
Join Your First Community
AI prompts user to read 3 posts from their chosen community group. Commenting is enabled immediately. Social belonging drives Day-7 retention by 3.2× (Woebot clinical data).
8
Step 8 · Notifications
Daily Check-In Setup
AI asks preferred check-in time ("When should Mira check on you?"). Sets local timezone push notification. Daily mood log = app stickiness engine. Streak counter starts.
9
Day 3 · Automated Email
First Weekly Insight Email
Make.com triggers personalised email: "Here's what your first 3 days show..." — AI-generated mood graph, 1 insight, 1 tip. Drives re-engagement for users who didn't open app Day 2–3.
10
Day 7 · Paywall Moment
Premium Upgrade Offer
After 7-day free trial, AI surfaces Premium screen: "You've had 12 conversations with Mira. Upgrade to keep going — $9.99/mo, cancel anytime." Stripe handles billing. No human involved.

Risk 01 — Regulatory
PIPEDA & Health Data Regulations

Mitigation: Canadian server-only data storage (AWS ca-central-1). No clinical claims made. Positioned as "wellness tool" not medical device. $12K Year 1 legal budget. Privacy policy drafted by Canadian health data counsel.

Risk 02 — Clinical Safety
AI Giving Harmful Advice

Mitigation: Claude API's built-in safety system. Custom system prompt with hard refusals for clinical advice. Automatic escalation to Canadian crisis lines. Part-time clinical advisor on retainer ($8K/yr) to review AI outputs monthly.

Risk 03 — Competition
Headspace/Calm Enter Canada AI

Mitigation: Canada-native social community is a moat they can't buy. Calm Health expanded to Canada June 2025 — B2B only, no social layer, no AI chat. Our social + AI combination is structurally different from their content library model.

Risk 04 — Retention
High Churn in Mental Health Apps

Mitigation: Social community creates lock-in (users don't leave communities). Streak gamification. Weekly AI-generated personalised insight emails. Day-7 and Day-30 re-engagement automations via Make.com. Target 60%+ Day-30 retention.
